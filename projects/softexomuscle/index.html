SoftExoMuscle

Jan 31, 2018

Overview

I Proposed the Brake system to reduce the stress on lumber. With this project, I aim to solve the lower back pain problem with this system, and I want to distribute to blue collar worker who suffering from LBP like care giver and This project aims to distribute this system as business product to blue-collar workers like care givers and deliver.
